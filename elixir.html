Let us drink the scrimba exilir to create design aspects that truly serves all users

Many sites on the web, including very well known, aren't always accessible to users with disabilities. The goal of our wonderful team is to design beautifully, but functionally acccording the WCGA standard. Coding and designing the web by accessibility standards, doesn't have to be boring. With great knowlegde of different aspects, you can do much with less. If you want more traffic to your site, better include those who often struggle to experience it fully

  • Colorblindess
  • Readability
  • Navigation

Different ways of building

Using plugins to check if your site is designed acccording the WCGA standards is a good start, but in the end you still need to code it and this is where we will shine.
